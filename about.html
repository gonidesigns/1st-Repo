A graphic designer with a passion for branding, web, print, and packaging design. I believe in the power of minimalism, joyful, and eco-friendly designs to improve people's lives.

I stay on top of industry advancements by learning to code and staying current with design trends. In my free time, I enjoy outdoor activities, baking, and mixology. I am always seeking new adventures and opportunities for growth.
