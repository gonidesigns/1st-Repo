Kitani Chalmee

Branding / Web Design

Kitani Chalmee is a Japanese physiotherapist serving Premier League players. The client requested a prominent display of his first name in the logo design.

I explored various concepts, including anatomy and shapes, to achieve a balanced design. The result is a simple, sophisticated logo featuring a harmonious monogram made up of supportive shapes.

I designed the website using Adobe XD and brought it to life with Webflow.
