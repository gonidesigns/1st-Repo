Grub Bites

Branding | Packaging Design

Grub Bites is a cricket flour snack brand aimed at the youthful, health-focused audience. These portable, nutrient-rich snacks are made from 100% insect flour and provide a boost of protein. Bright colours and engaging illustrations serve to not only draw the eye, but also educate on the benefits of sustainable living. Satisfy your hunger and do your part for the planet with Grub Bites.
