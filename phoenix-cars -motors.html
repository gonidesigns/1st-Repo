Phoenix, Cars & Motors

Logo Design

Phoenix, Cars & Motors is a start-up automotive dealership located in Bath. Their passion is to rebuild scrap vehicles and bring them back to life with a spark.

The bold, yet elegant, lines create the presence of a Phoenix rising from the ashes. The Pixels represented the company's vision for a video series. This had been created with a strong visual connection to a vehicle's grille and headlights.
